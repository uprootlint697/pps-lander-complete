Cleaning your machine removes the buildup inside your drum. That's important. But it doesn't
change what happens during the rest of your laundry cycle.

Pet odor and hair doesn't just live in your machine.

Inside Your Machine

Solved

Pet hair, dander, and moisture collect in your drum, gasket, and drain lines after every load. Over time this creates a layer of mold and bacteria that makes your "clean" clothes smell worse than your dog's bed.
